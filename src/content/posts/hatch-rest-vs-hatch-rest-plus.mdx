## What to Know Before You Buy

No recalls on either model. No flagged materials. Both are BPA-free and meet standard safety certifications for nursery use.

One practical note on the Rest+: the battery runs approximately 13 hours on a full charge. Long enough for naps and most of an overnight stretch. Hatch recommends keeping it plugged in overnight so it stays topped up. If you are expecting fully cordless overnight performance indefinitely, recalibrate that expectation now.

Both models require Wi-Fi and the Hatch app to customize sounds, light color, and routines. If app-dependent baby gear is not your thing on principle, the LectroFan below is the right call. No app, no account, no Wi-Fi dependency.
