## How to Use This Map

Every post belongs to one of six pillars. Each pillar has one authoritative pillar page supported by cluster pages that link back to it. Before drafting any post, identify its pillar and confirm the internal link.

**Status markers:** (Live) = published | (In Progress) = currently drafting | (Planned) = in queue

**Seasonal priority:** Posts marked [NOW] are the current priority based on April 2026.

**Content types:**
- **Comparison** = "X vs Y: Which One Is Worth It?" — highest commercial value, target first
- **Best-of** = pillar page or "Best [Category]" — broad traffic, multiple price points
- **Skip This** = explicit negative recommendation — highest trust-building content on the site
- **Registry Reality** = what you actually need vs what you think you need — highest email signup driver
- **Problem-Solution** = pain point + product recommendation
- **Safety** = recall coverage, safety standards, research explainers

**Priority format within each pillar:** Comparison posts first, then Skip This, then Best-of.

**Your first three posts (builds authority across three pillars simultaneously):**
1. Hatch Rest vs Hatch Rest+: Which One Is Worth It? — Sleep (Comparison)
2. Ergobaby vs Solly Baby Wrap: Which One Is Worth It? — Carriers and Strollers (Comparison)
3. Nanit vs Owlet: Which Baby Monitor Is Worth It? — Safety (Comparison)
